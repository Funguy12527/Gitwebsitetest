Ergonomic Keyboard

  • After experiencing persistent hand discomfort from long typing sessions, I realized my conventional keyboard was a primary contributor to the strain. The unnatural, fixed posture it enforced made it clear that a more ergonomic solution was essential for my long-term health and comfort. This need directly inspired and motivated my project to build this fully customizable, split keyboard, allowing me to position each half perfectly to maintain a neutral and relaxed typing position.
